Privacy Policy

At VECTROX Security Services, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

Interpretation & Definitions

The terms used throughout this Privacy Policy have specific meanings and are intended to provide clarity regarding the collection and use of personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Name and contact details
  • Email address and telephone number
  • Company information
  • Website usage and analytics data
  • Service enquiry information

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Respond to enquiries and quotations
  • Manage client relationships
  • Improve website functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience, analyse website traffic, and enhance website performance.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact VECTROX Security Services.
