Workplace Pension Policy

Supporting Your Financial Future

At VECTROX Security Services, we recognise the importance of helping employees prepare for a secure and comfortable retirement. Our Workplace Pension Scheme has been established to support long-term financial wellbeing and to ensure compliance with UK workplace pension legislation.

Through automatic enrolment, eligible employees are enrolled into our approved workplace pension scheme, providing an easy and effective way to begin saving for retirement while benefiting from employer contributions.

Automatic Enrolment

Eligible employees are automatically enrolled into the workplace pension scheme.

Employer Contributions

VECTROX contributes towards employee pension savings to support retirement planning.

Long-Term Growth

Contributions are invested to help build retirement savings over time.

Financial Security

Helping employees achieve greater financial confidence for the future.

Automatic Enrolment Eligibility

Employees may be automatically enrolled into the workplace pension scheme if they:

  • Meet the age requirements set by current UK pension regulations.
  • Earn above the minimum qualifying earnings threshold.
  • Work within the United Kingdom.
  • Are not already participating in a qualifying workplace pension scheme.

Benefits of the Pension Scheme

  • Employer pension contributions.
  • Tax-efficient retirement savings.
  • Long-term investment growth opportunities.
  • Flexible retirement planning options.
  • Support for long-term financial wellbeing.

Employee Contributions

Employees who are enrolled into the pension scheme contribute a percentage of their earnings towards their retirement savings. These contributions are combined with employer contributions and any applicable tax relief, helping to maximise long-term pension growth.

Employees who are not automatically enrolled may still have the right to join the scheme, subject to pension regulations and eligibility criteria.

Our Commitment

VECTROX Security Services is committed to supporting the financial wellbeing of our workforce. We believe that providing access to a quality workplace pension scheme demonstrates our commitment to employee welfare, long-term career development, and future financial security.

VECTROX Security Services delivers professional security solutions across the United Kingdom, providing Security Guarding, Mobile Patrols, Construction Security, Corporate Security, Concierge Security, Event Security, CCTV Monitoring, and Key Holding services backed by experienced personnel and exceptional customer service.
