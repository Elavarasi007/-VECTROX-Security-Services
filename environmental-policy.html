Environmental Policy

Our Environmental Commitment

VECTROX Security Services recognises the importance of protecting the environment and reducing the impact of our operations on the communities we serve. We are committed to conducting our business responsibly, complying with environmental legislation, and promoting sustainable practices throughout our organisation.

Through effective environmental management, continuous improvement, and responsible decision-making, we aim to minimise waste, reduce energy consumption, and support a cleaner, safer, and more sustainable future.

Legal Compliance

Full compliance with environmental legislation, regulations, and industry best practices.

Energy Efficiency

Reducing energy consumption through responsible operational planning and monitoring.

Waste Reduction

Promoting recycling and reducing waste wherever practical.

Environmental Objectives

  • Reduce environmental impact across all business activities.
  • Promote sustainable resource management.
  • Improve environmental awareness among employees.
  • Prevent pollution wherever possible.
  • Encourage environmentally responsible practices.
  • Support continuous environmental improvement.

Energy & Climate Change

We recognise the challenges posed by climate change and are committed to reducing our environmental footprint. VECTROX Security Services continually evaluates opportunities to improve energy efficiency, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, and implement environmentally responsible solutions throughout our operations.

Pollution Prevention

We aim to minimise pollution and environmental risks by implementing effective operational controls, promoting sustainable working practices, and ensuring all employees understand their environmental responsibilities.

Monitoring & Continuous Improvement

Environmental performance is regularly monitored through audits, reviews, and management processes. We continually assess our objectives and targets to ensure ongoing compliance and measurable environmental improvements.

Our Environmental Promise

VECTROX Security Services is committed to protecting the environment, conserving resources, reducing waste, and continuously improving environmental performance while delivering exceptional security services across the United Kingdom.

VECTROX Security Services delivers professional security solutions across the United Kingdom, providing Security Guarding, Mobile Patrols, Construction Security, Corporate Security, Concierge Security, Event Security, CCTV Monitoring, and Key Holding services backed by experienced personnel and exceptional customer service.
