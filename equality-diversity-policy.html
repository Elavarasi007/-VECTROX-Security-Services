POLICY FOR EQUAL OPPORTUNITIES AND DIVERSITY

Equality & Diversity Policy

Equality & Diversity Commitment

At VECTROX Security Services, we are committed to promoting equality, diversity, inclusion, and respect throughout every aspect of our business. We recognise that a diverse workforce strengthens our organisation and enables us to provide better services to our clients and communities.

We aim to create a working environment where all individuals are treated fairly, with dignity and respect, regardless of their background, identity, beliefs, or personal circumstances. We actively oppose all forms of discrimination, harassment, bullying, and victimisation.

Gender

Equal opportunities and fair treatment regardless of gender or gender identity.

Disability

Supporting accessibility and making reasonable workplace adjustments.

Race & Ethnicity

Respecting all cultures, backgrounds, nationalities, and ethnic origins.

Inclusion

Creating a welcoming environment where everyone feels valued.

Policy Objectives

Our Equality & Diversity Policy aims to ensure every individual has equal access to employment, development opportunities, training, promotion, and workplace benefits without discrimination.

  • Promote equality and eliminate unlawful discrimination.
  • Create an inclusive and supportive workplace culture.
  • Ensure equal opportunities throughout employment.
  • Encourage diversity at all levels of the organisation.
  • Comply fully with the Equality Act 2010.
  • Provide fair and transparent recruitment processes.

Types of Discrimination

VECTROX Security Services does not tolerate any form of unlawful discrimination. This includes direct discrimination, indirect discrimination, harassment, victimisation, bullying, or any unfair treatment based on protected characteristics.

  • Direct Discrimination – Treating someone less favourably because of a protected characteristic.
  • Indirect Discrimination – Applying policies that unfairly disadvantage specific groups.
  • Harassment – Unwanted behaviour that violates dignity or creates a hostile environment.
  • Victimisation – Unfair treatment because someone has raised a discrimination concern.

Fair Recruitment & Employment

Recruitment, promotion, training, and career development decisions are based solely on merit, qualifications, experience, and business requirements. We actively seek to attract and retain talented individuals from diverse backgrounds.

We are committed to maintaining recruitment procedures that are fair, transparent, and free from bias, ensuring equal opportunities for all applicants and employees.

Reasonable Adjustments

We recognise our responsibility to provide appropriate support to employees and applicants with disabilities. Where reasonable and practical, adjustments will be made to ensure equal access and opportunities.

  • Accessible working environments.
  • Adapted workstations and equipment.
  • Flexible working arrangements where appropriate.
  • Additional training and support provisions.
  • Modified duties where required and practical.

Responsibilities

Every employee, manager, contractor, and stakeholder associated with VECTROX Security Services shares responsibility for maintaining a respectful and inclusive working environment.

  • Treat colleagues, clients, and visitors with dignity and respect.
  • Challenge discriminatory behaviour when encountered.
  • Promote equality and inclusion in daily operations.
  • Report incidents of discrimination or harassment.
  • Support organisational diversity initiatives.

Our Commitment

VECTROX Security Services is dedicated to fostering an inclusive workplace where every individual feels respected, valued, and empowered to achieve their full potential. By embracing diversity and promoting equality, we strengthen our organisation, improve our services, and contribute positively to the communities we serve throughout the United Kingdom.

VECTROX Security Services delivers professional security solutions across the United Kingdom, providing Security Guarding, Mobile Patrols, Construction Security, Corporate Security, Concierge Security, Event Security, CCTV Monitoring, Key Holding, and specialist security services backed by experienced personnel and exceptional customer service.
