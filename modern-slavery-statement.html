Anti-Modern Slavery Statement

Our Commitment

VECTROX Security Services is committed to preventing modern slavery, forced labour, servitude, human trafficking, and all forms of exploitation within our business operations and supply chains. We recognise our responsibility to uphold human rights and maintain ethical business practices across all areas of our organisation.

We operate a zero-tolerance approach to modern slavery and are committed to acting with integrity, transparency, and accountability in all business relationships.

Ethical Conduct

Maintaining the highest standards of integrity and professionalism.

Human Rights

Protecting the rights and dignity of all workers.

Supply Chain

Monitoring suppliers to ensure ethical employment practices.

Compliance

Operating in accordance with UK legislation and regulations.

Organisation & Supply Chains

VECTROX Security Services provides professional security solutions throughout the United Kingdom. Our operations are supported by trusted suppliers, contractors, and service providers who are expected to uphold the same ethical standards that we apply within our own organisation.

  • Commitment to lawful and ethical recruitment practices.
  • Regular supplier evaluations and compliance checks.
  • Transparent employment procedures.
  • Fair treatment of all employees and contractors.

Responsibility

Senior management is responsible for ensuring compliance with this policy and for implementing effective controls designed to prevent modern slavery within our operations and supply chain.

Due Diligence

  • Assessment of supplier risk levels.
  • Verification of employment standards.
  • Regular compliance monitoring.
  • Investigation of reported concerns.
  • Corrective action where necessary.

Training & Awareness

Employees receive guidance and training to help identify potential indicators of modern slavery and human trafficking. This ensures that concerns can be recognised and reported appropriately.

Zero-Tolerance Commitment

VECTROX Security Services remains committed to combating modern slavery and human trafficking in all forms. We continually review our policies, procedures, and supplier relationships to ensure the highest standards of ethical conduct and human rights protection.

VECTROX Security Services delivers professional security solutions across the United Kingdom, providing Security Guarding, Mobile Patrols, Construction Security, Corporate Security, Concierge Security, Event Security, CCTV Monitoring, Key Holding, and specialist security services backed by experienced personnel and exceptional customer service.
