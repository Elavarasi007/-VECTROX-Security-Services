Workplace Drug & Alcohol Policy

Purpose of this Policy

At VECTROX Security Services, the health, safety, and wellbeing of our employees, clients, contractors, and members of the public remain a top priority. The misuse of alcohol or drugs can significantly impair judgement, performance, and reaction times, creating serious risks within the workplace.

This policy outlines our commitment to maintaining a safe, professional, and productive working environment by establishing clear standards regarding alcohol and drug use and providing support to employees who may require assistance.

Safety First

Protecting employees, clients, and the public from avoidable risks.

Fair Treatment

Ensuring all staff are treated consistently and respectfully.

Support Available

Encouraging employees to seek help whenever required.

Legal Compliance

Operating in line with UK legislation and industry standards.

Key Principles

  • Promote a safe and healthy working environment.
  • Prevent accidents and incidents caused by impairment.
  • Support employees who voluntarily seek assistance.
  • Ensure confidentiality wherever appropriate.
  • Apply policies fairly and consistently across the organisation.
  • Comply with all relevant UK legislation and regulations.

Scope of the Policy

This policy applies to all employees, agency workers, contractors, temporary staff, and any individuals undertaking work on behalf of VECTROX Security Services.

  • Working hours and shifts.
  • Company premises and client locations.
  • Business travel and work-related events.
  • Use of company vehicles and equipment.

Workplace Rules

  • Employees must not report for work under the influence of alcohol or illegal drugs.
  • Possession, use, distribution, or supply of illegal substances is strictly prohibited.
  • Alcohol must not be consumed during working hours unless specifically authorised.
  • Prescription medication that may affect performance should be reported to management.
  • Any employee suspected of impairment may be subject to investigation in accordance with company procedures.

Legal Obligations

VECTROX Security Services is committed to complying with all applicable legal requirements relating to workplace health and safety, substance misuse, and employee welfare.

  • Health and Safety at Work Act 1974.
  • Misuse of Drugs Act 1971.
  • Road Traffic Act requirements.
  • Employment and equality legislation.

Help & Support

Employees experiencing difficulties related to alcohol or substance misuse are encouraged to seek support as early as possible. VECTROX Security Services will provide appropriate assistance and referrals wherever practical.

  • Confidential discussions with management.
  • Occupational health referrals.
  • Professional counselling services.
  • Guidance from healthcare providers.

Useful Support Organisations

  • Alcoholics Anonymous (AA)
  • FRANK
  • NHS Support Services
  • Alcohol Change UK

Our Commitment

VECTROX Security Services is committed to maintaining a workplace free from the risks associated with alcohol and drug misuse. Through awareness, support, training, and responsible management practices, we aim to safeguard our employees, clients, and the communities we serve.

VECTROX Security Services delivers professional security solutions across the United Kingdom, providing Security Guarding, Mobile Patrols, Construction Security, Corporate Security, Concierge Security, Event Security, CCTV Monitoring, and Key Holding services backed by experienced personnel and exceptional customer service.
