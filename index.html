Guard Patrol Monitoring System

We also use a guard patrol monitoring system which is an innovative and easy-to-use security guard tracking solution. It enables you to:

  • Show proof of attendance at the site.
  • Show evidence of patrolling using NFC tags installed around the site.
  • Send incident reports instantly.
  • Take and send pictures of incidents.
  • Raise an automatic alarm in the control room for missed patrols.
